The Story Behind CineXpress

CineXpress began with a simple idea — make the joy of watching movies easy to reach. No endless queues, no confusing bookings — just a smooth, fast, and fun way to catch your favourite films.

Built in 2025 by a group of cinema lovers and tech enthusiasts, CineXpress blends simplicity with smart design. Whether it's the latest blockbuster or a local gem, we help connect you to theatres with just a few taps.

Our goal is to keep movie experiences stress-free — for fans and for theatre owners alike.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make discovering and booking movies easy and enjoyable for everyone — bringing entertainment closer to home.

Our Vision

To become India's most loved movie ticketing platform by bringing technology, theatres, and fans together under one roof.
